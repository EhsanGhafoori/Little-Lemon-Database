# ER Diagram Notes

Add your MySQL Workbench ER diagram PNG in this folder as:

- `little_lemon_er_diagram.png`

The ER diagram should show at least:

- Customers -> Bookings (1 to many)
- Bookings -> Orders (1 to many)

You can also include additional operational tables (Staff, MenuItems, OrderDeliveryStatus) if your course version expects them.
